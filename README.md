# Odock Landing

Odock is the AI governance gateway for organizations adopting large language models and MCP-based workflows.

It helps teams connect, monitor, and control AI activity across their tools, models, and agents, giving businesses the visibility and confidence they need to scale AI safely.

## What Odock Does

Odock provides a centralized gateway for managing AI interactions across the organization. It helps teams understand how AI is being used, apply governance policies, reduce risk, and maintain control over sensitive data and model access.

## Key Value

Odock enables companies to:

* Govern AI usage across teams and workflows
* Improve visibility into LLM and MCP traffic
* Strengthen security and compliance
* Manage access, policies, and permissions
* Support multilingual teams with English and French experiences
* Present product, pricing, security, plugin, blog, and contact content in a clear customer-facing experience

## Positioning

Odock is built for teams that want to move fast with AI while keeping governance, security, and oversight in place.

It acts as a trusted control layer between users, applications, AI models, and connected tools, helping organizations adopt AI responsibly without slowing innovation.

## Website Experience

The Odock landing site is designed to communicate the product clearly to business, security, and technology decision-makers. It includes sections for:

* Product overview
* Pricing
* Security
* Plugins and integrations
* Blog and thought leadership
* Contact and lead generation
* Legal and trust pages

## Message

Odock helps organizations scale AI adoption with confidence by bringing governance, visibility, and control to every AI interaction.
